The world is our office.

Our Theme

The world is your oyster our office.

We started the program during the fall semester of 2020 in the midst of a global pandemic. Being the first cohort of New Media Arts to work remotely from the very beginning to completion, we learned how to adapt to the changes going on in the world. Making the best out of the situation and being able to work from various locations, we made the world our office.

An asynchronous event

What does this mean?

This is not a live event. On the day of the launch and thereafter, you will be able to watch our student show presentation at your own convenience; so pause, rewind, and fastforward as you please!

About KCC New Media Arts

KCC New Media Arts (NMA) is a two year AS degree program located at Kapiolani Community College in Honolulu, Hawaii. The NMA mission is to prepare students for employment in the fields of interface design and animation.

Gratitude

As a cohort, we would like to give our special thanks to KCC administration and staff for helping us get through the past two unpredictable years. We are forever grateful for all the knowledge and support you have poured into us as we proceed with confidence into our own journeys. With your guidance, you have helped us to broaden our horizons, help us to realize our capabilities, and what we can be capable of. Through the peaks and pits of remote work, together, we have found the silver lining. The world is our office. Mahalo Nui Loa!
